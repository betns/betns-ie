## **Welcome to Blessington Educate Together National School**

Blessington Educate Together National School is a multi-denominational primary school in Wicklow. We provide a co-educational, child-centred, and democratically-run learning environment. Our focus is on inclusive education, ensuring that every child thrives academically, socially, and emotionally.

Located 2km from Blessington Town, our school sits in a scenic area near Blessington Lakes, offering a beautiful and peaceful setting for learning. With four mainstream classes and two special education classes, we cater to approximately 100 pupils, fostering a strong sense of community and belonging.

### **A School Designed for Growth & Well-being**

Our spacious 2.5-acre grounds include:\
✔ Expansive green areas for outdoor learning & play\
✔ Dedicated sensory spaces for student well-being\
✔ Active play zones\
✔ A thriving school garden to encourage sustainability & hands-on learning

At Blessington Educate Together, collaboration is at our core. The Board of Management, staff, parents, and students work together to create a nurturing and progressive educational environment.
