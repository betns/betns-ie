See below for the 2025 school year calendar for Blessington Educate Together National School. This page is the place to stay up to date with important term dates, school events, activities, and community celebrations.

As an Educate Together school, our calendar reflects not only the rhythm of the academic year but also our ethos of equality, respect, inclusion, and active learning. From holiday schedules to special events that celebrate diversity and community, our school calendar helps families stay connected and informed throughout the year.

**Following on from the publication of this year’s school calendar, the school has been informed of the following school closure dates by the Department of Education and Youth:**

- **07/11/2025 School closed owing to Child Protection Training for School Staff**
- **09/02/2026  School closed due to Teacher Training for the New Primary Curriculum Framework (Day 1)**
- **11/05/2026  School closed due to Teacher Training for the New Primary Curriculum Framework (Day 2)**
